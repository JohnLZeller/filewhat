A Cheffile.lock is a file that is generated by librarian-chef when using Chef, and represents the cookbooks/recipes you need for your app based on what librarian-chef previously ran. Chef is a powerful automation platform that transforms infrastructure into code by automating how infrastructure is configured, deployed, and managed across your network, no matter its size. librarian-chef is a tool that helps you manage the cookbooks that your chef-repo depends on.
